Presentations

I’ve presented almost 200 talks on technology and its use in libraries of all types. In addition to training within libraries, I’ve presented online and in person at national, state, and regional events. Topics include web development, cloud computing, open source, mobile devices/gadgets, social networking, and general technology trends.
